# User Manual

## Running an analysis (no account needed)

1. Open the app and press **Start analysis** (or *Products* → browse first).
2. **Consent** — read what happens with your photo. Processing is temporary by default; the "save my analysis image" box is optional and off. Tick the agreement to continue.
3. **Guidance** — the *Aim for / Avoid* lists matter: natural daylight, no filters, no heavy makeup, forehead and cheeks visible, one person only.
4. **Photo** — two tabs:
   - **Camera**: press *Enable camera* (the browser asks for permission — it starts only when you ask, and no video is recorded). Fit your face inside the oval and press *Take photo*. Multiple cameras? Use *Switch camera*.
   - **Upload**: drag a JPEG/PNG/WebP (≤10 MB) or browse. If the camera is unavailable or you decline permission, the app moves you here automatically.
5. **Preview** — retake freely; when happy press **Analyse this photo**.
6. If the photo can't be analysed reliably (too dark, blurred, several faces, strong colour cast…), you'll get a specific explanation and retake tips rather than a shaky result.

## Reading your result

- **Headline**: estimated undertone (warm/cool) and your suggested season — a sub-season (e.g. *Deep Autumn*) appears only when confidence is high enough to be useful.
- **Confidence badge**: high / medium / low with a percentage. Low confidence is an honest invitation to retake in better light, not a failure.
- **Why this result**: the measured evidence (hue angle, yellowness, depth, region agreement) in plain language.
- **Your colours to explore**: the full palette in groups — neutrals, core, accents, formal, casual, accessories, **hijab & headwear**, and *Use with care* (less harmonious colours with gentler alternatives — never "forbidden"). Tap any swatch code to copy the HEX; press **Print palette card** for a printable version.
- **Cosmetic directions**: lipstick/blusher/eyeshadow/eyeliner/highlighter families plus a foundation *undertone direction* only — always test makeup in daylight.

## With an account

Register (or sign in) to unlock:

- **Auto-saved analyses** — every signed-in analysis lands in *Saved analyses*; open one for the full detail: measured skin colours per region, styling dimensions, palette with favourite hearts, and **Products for your palette** ranked by colour match.
- **Save your photo** — only if you choose: tick the consent option, or press *Also save my photo* on the result. Stored privately; view or delete it from the analysis detail.
- **Favourites** — heart any palette colour or product; find them under *Favourites*.
- **Settings → Privacy** — set the storage default, delete your complete history, or delete your account (everything you own is removed, permanently).

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | What to do |
|---|---|
| Camera never opens | Check the browser's site permissions; the page must be HTTPS. Upload works regardless. |
| "No face was detected" | More light, face the camera, come closer, remove obstructions. |
| "Multiple faces" | Be the only person in the frame (posters/photos in the background count!). |
| Colour-cast warning | Move away from warm-white/coloured bulbs; daylight from a window is best. |
| Result feels off | Check the confidence label and the disclaimer page — lighting and filters shift measurements; retake in daylight without makeup. |
